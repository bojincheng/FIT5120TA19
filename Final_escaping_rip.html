How to Escape a Rip Current

  1. Don't panic - Stay calm and conserve your energy.
  2. Don't swim against the current - You'll quickly become exhausted.
  3. Swim parallel to the shore - Move sideways (left or right) to escape the narrow rip current.
  4. Once free, swim at an angle back to shore - Use the incoming waves to help you return.
  5. If you can't escape - Float or tread water and call for help. Wave your arms to attract attention.
